Welcome to Orb Studios

We are a new rehearsal and recording space in Etobicoke, Ontario. We offer a private yet personal space for bands and artists to rent on either a monthly or hourly basis to create music. With a collection of gear on site, or the option for gear storage for our monthly rental customers, Orb Studios is the place to hole up and create your next masterpiece.

Studio A Rehearsal Room

21 x 17 x 11 foot space with following gear included:
  • DW Drumkit with assorted cymbals and hardware.
  • Fender 100W Rumble Bass Amp
  • Orange Guitar Amp/Cab Combo
  • Fender '65 Deluxe Reverb
  • Allen & Heath ZED 4 Channel Mixer
  • Yorkville PA Speakers
  • Assorted Dynamic Mics (57s, 58s, 421, d112s, etc.)

Mixing / Control Room

12.5 x 10.5 x 8 foot space with following capabilities:
  • Simple 2 Track Recording of Studio A Rehearsal.
  • Full Analogue to Digital recording up to 32 channels.
  • Pro Tools / Logic / Reaper / Audacity / Ableton 10 Lite / GarageBand / Cubase LE / Studio One
  • 88 Weighted Key Midi Keyboard and Native Instruments Komplete (with Maschine).
  • Allen & Heath ZED 4 Channel Mixer
  • VSX Headphone System
  • Assorted Mics (Dyanmics, Condensers, Ribbons, Emulation)

Amenities

Gear Loading and Unloading Zone Available. Free Parking available on first come first serve basis. Street and Paid parking nearby.

For monthly rentals gear storage is available. We store amps, drums, microphones, etc. We will have digital door locks and key locks for storage, as well as a security camera system on both the gear storage and lounge.

A very large lounge is privately available during your sessions with tvs, streaming services, kitchen and washroom access (including shower). Keurig Coffee machine, refrigerator, music sound system, etc.

A great area of Mimico with nearby coffee shops, pizza places, diner, bakeries, etc. There are also a couple nearby sit down restaurants and convenience stores. All within walking distance.

Mimico Go station is a 5 minute walk, 2 stops from Union Station (15-30 minute intervals on weekdays). There is also the nearby TTC 76B bus which runs along Royal York. We are also a 10 minute drive from the Royal York Subway Station.
